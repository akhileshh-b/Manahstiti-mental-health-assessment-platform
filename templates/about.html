About Manahstiti

Manahstiti is an anonymous mental health assessment platform designed specifically for students, providing scientifically-backed mental health evaluations and personalized support resources.

Our Mission

To provide students with accessible, anonymous, and scientifically-validated mental health assessments that empower them to understand their mental wellbeing and connect with appropriate support resources. We believe that mental health awareness and early intervention are crucial for student success and overall wellbeing.

What Makes Us Different

Complete Anonymity

No personal data collection, no login required. Your privacy is our top priority with GDPR-compliant practices.

Science-Backed Assessment

Our 50-question assessment uses machine learning with 99.17% accuracy, trained on validated mental health data.

Student-Focused

Specifically designed for university students, addressing unique challenges like academic stress and social pressures.

AI-Powered Support

Chat with our empathetic AI friend for immediate emotional support and guidance whenever you need it.

How Our Assessment Works

1

Comprehensive Questions

Technical Foundation

Assessment Dimensions

  • • Mood and emotional well-being (10 questions)
  • • Stress and pressure levels (10 questions)
  • • Sleep quality and patterns (10 questions)
  • • Social connections and support (10 questions)
  • • Academic pressure and performance (10 questions)

Classification System

  • • Good Mental Health: Score 200-250
  • • Moderate Concerns: Score 125-199
  • • Poor Mental Health: Score 50-124
  • • 99.17% accuracy on validation dataset
  • • Based on 1M+ mental health assessments
