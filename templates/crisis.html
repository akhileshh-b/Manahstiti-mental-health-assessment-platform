🧘 Immediate Steps You Can Take

Call or WhatsApp a helpline — even if you're unsure what to say.

Use the 5-4-3-2-1 grounding technique if you're panicking:

5 things you can see • 4 things you can touch • 3 things you hear • 2 things you smell • 1 thing you taste

(Breathe slowly while doing this.)

Tell someone you trust. A friend, family member, or teacher.

Remove yourself from immediate danger or harmful situations.

🧾 What You Can Say

When you call a helpline, you don't need to explain everything. You can start with:

💬

"I'm feeling really overwhelmed and need someone to talk to."

💬

"I don't feel okay. Can you help me?"

💬

"I'm having thoughts of hurting myself."

They'll take it from there. No judgment. Just help.
